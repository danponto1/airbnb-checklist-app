# Airbnb Checklist App – Requirements (Draft)

## Context
- Business: residential/commercial cleaning company
- Need to recreate Breezeway checklist workflow without giving cleaners Breezeway access
- Two Airbnb properties, separate forms preferred

## Form behavior decisions (confirmed)
1. Checklist items use **checkbox complete** (no pass/fail)
2. Any item that indicates a problem/issue should require:
   - issue note
   - issue photo
3. Kitchen restock quantity: **3 paper towel rolls**
4. Laundry room washer/dryer requires a photo
5. Bedrooms require **one photo each**

## Checklist sections
- Interior
- Exterior
- Before You Leave

## MVP app scope
- Cleaner submission form(s)
- Photo upload support
- Admin view for submitted forms + photos
- Filter by property/date/cleaner
